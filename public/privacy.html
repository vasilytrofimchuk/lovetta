Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up for the waitlist or use our service, we collect:

  • Account information: Email address, month and year of birth (for age verification)
  • Usage data: Device type, screen resolution, language, timezone, pages visited
  • Location data: Approximate location derived from your IP address (country, state, city)
  • Marketing data: Referral source, UTM campaign parameters

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To communicate with you about our service and your waitlist status
  • To verify that you meet the minimum age requirement (18+)
  • To improve our service through analytics
  • To send you updates about Lovetta (you can opt out at any time)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use a cloud hosting provider with data centers that comply with SOC 2 standards. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use session storage (not persistent cookies) to track your visit within a single browser session. This data is used solely for analytics and is not shared with advertisers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

6. Age Requirement

Lovetta is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We collect your month and year of birth to verify eligibility. If we determine that a user is under 18, their data will be deleted.

7. AI-Generated Content

Our service uses artificial intelligence to generate text and images. Conversations with AI companions are processed by our AI service providers to generate responses. We may store conversation history to improve the companion's memory and response quality.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@lovetta.ai.
